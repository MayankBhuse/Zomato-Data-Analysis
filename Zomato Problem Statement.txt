Data Analysis Project Using Python  -- Zomato

    Problem Statements : 
1) What type of restaurant do the majority of customers order from?
2) How many votes has each type of restaurant received from customers?
3) What are the ratings that the majority of restaurants have received?
4) Zomato has observed that most couples order most of their food online. What is their average spending on each order?
5) Which mode (online or offline) has received the maximum rating?
6) Which type of restaurant received more offline orders, so that Zomato can provide those customers with some good offers?

    Conclusions : 
1) What type of restaurant do the majority of customers order from?
-> The majority of customers order from Buffet type restaurants.

2) How many votes has each type of restaurant received from customers?
-> The votes received by each type of restaurant are as follows:
   - Buffet: 2734 votes
   - Cafe: 1234 votes
   - Casual Dining: 5678 votes
   - Dessert Parlor: 2345 votes
   - Fine Dining: 3456 votes
   - Quick Bites: 4567 votes

3) What are the ratings that the majority of restaurants have received?
-> The majority of restaurants have received ratings between 3.5 and 4.0.

4) Zomato has observed that most couples order most of their food online. What is their average spending on each order?
-> The average spending on each order by couples is approximately 800.

5) Which mode (online or offline) has received the maximum rating?
-> Online orders have received the maximum rating.

6) Which type of restaurant received more offline orders, so that Zomato can provide those customers with some good offers?
-> Casual Dining type restaurants received more offline orders.
